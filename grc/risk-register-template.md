## Scoring Notes

- Likelihood: Low, Medium, High.
- Impact: Low, Medium, High.
- Risk Rating: combine likelihood and impact, then adjust based on exposure and compensating controls.
- Recommendation: write an action that can be assigned, tracked, and verified.
