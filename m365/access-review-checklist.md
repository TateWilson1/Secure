# Microsoft 365 / Entra Access Review Checklist

This checklist supports identity and access review practice and aligns with current SC-300 study.

## Review Areas

- Stale users, inactive accounts, and disabled accounts with lingering access.
- Privileged roles and role assignment justification.
- MFA registration and conditional access coverage.
- Guest users and external collaboration settings.
- Enterprise applications, app consent, SSO configuration, and unused app registrations.
- Shared mailboxes, groups, and Teams ownership.
- Break-glass account presence, protection, and monitoring.

## Output Format

For each finding, capture:

- Observation.
- Risk.
- Evidence.
- Recommended action.
- Owner.
- Priority.
- Follow-up date.
